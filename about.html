About Grid Escape

Practical off-grid guides, without the noise.

Grid Escape is an independent site covering off-grid power, solar energy, camping, and van life — built to answer the questions we found ourselves googling at 11pm before a trip.

Why This Site Exists

Off-grid power has a steep learning curve. Between watt-hours, amp-hours, charge controllers, and a flood of gear reviews that all seem to disagree with each other, it's easy to spend more time researching than actually getting outside or on the road.

Grid Escape started as a way to write down clear, practical answers to the questions that kept coming up — how solar systems actually work, how to size a battery for a weekend trip, what a van electrical build really needs. The goal is simple: explain things the way we wish they'd been explained to us the first time.

How We Approach Content

Grid Escape earns money through affiliate commissions when readers buy products through certain links on the site — full details are on our affiliate disclosure page. That relationship never changes what gets written or how a topic is explained.

Plain Explanations

No unnecessary jargon. If a term needs a specialized dictionary to understand, we're not doing our job.

Practical First

Every guide is written to answer a real decision someone's trying to make, not to pad out a word count.

Honest About What We Know

This is a growing, independent site. We'd rather say "we're not sure" than present a guess as certainty.
